**Improvising Damage**

| `dice:2d10\|noform\|noparens\|avg` (`2d10`) | Being struck by lightning, stumbling into a fire pit |
| `dice:4d10\|noform\|noparens\|avg` (`4d10`) | Hit by falling rubble in a collapsing tunnel, stumbling into a vat of acid |
