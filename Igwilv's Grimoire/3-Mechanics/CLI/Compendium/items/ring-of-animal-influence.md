# Ring of Animal Influence
*Ring, major, rare*  
![](/3-Mechanics/CLI/Compendium/items/img/ring-of-animal-influence.webp#right)


This ring has 3 charges, and it regains `dice:1d3|noform|noparens|avg` (`d3`) expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing the ring, you can use an action to expend 1 of its charges to cast one of the following spells:

- [Animal friendship](/3-Mechanics/CLI/Compendium/spells/animal-friendship.md) (save DC 13)  
- [Fear](/3-Mechanics/CLI/Compendium/spells/fear.md) (save DC 13), targeting only beasts that have an Intelligence of 3 or lower  
- [Speak with animals](/3-Mechanics/CLI/Compendium/spells/speak-with-animals.md)  

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 189. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
