# Resistance
*cantrip, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, a miniature cloak
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You touch one willing creature. Once before the spell ends, the target can roll a `d4` and add the number rolled to one saving throw of its choice. It can roll the die before or after making the saving throw. The spell then ends.

**Classes**: [Artificer](list-spells-classes-artificer); [Bard](list-spells-classes-bard); [Cleric](list-spells-classes-cleric); [Druid](list-spells-classes-druid); [Druid (Circle of the Land)](list-spells-classes-druid-circle-of-the-land); [Sorcerer (Divine Soul)](list-spells-classes-sorcerer-divine-soul-xge); [Cleric (Nature Domain)](list-spells-classes-cleric-nature-domain)
