# Divine Favor
*1st-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 bonus
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Your prayer empowers you with divine radiance. Until the spell ends, your weapon attacks deal an extra `1d4` radiant damage on a hit.

**Classes**: [Bard](list-spells-classes-bard); [Paladin](list-spells-classes-paladin); [Cleric (War Domain)](list-spells-classes-cleric-war-domain)

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 234. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
